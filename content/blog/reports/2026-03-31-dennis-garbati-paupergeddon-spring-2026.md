## Introduction

Hi guys!!
I'm Pizzardone (aka Dennis to my enemies) and I finished 29th out of 981 players in Paupergeddon Spring 2026 with Mono U Terror.

Unfortunately, this deck sucks in a metagame with high variance like this tournament, especially with so many Elves and the new Affinity around. I tuned the list to win a lot of Game 1 and Game 3 on the play. Force Spike is really strong against mostly all the pre-sideboard meta; post-side I usually switch with specific counters like Annul and Envelop.

It was an insane and difficult run, but I'm satisfied with the list and how I played all weekend.

The best advice I can write (for anyone who wants to try this aggressive build) is not to play Deem Inferior. Instead, I opted for a 2 Sleep / 1 Artful Dodge split (which was incredible as always) and played six one-mana counters in the main deck for the maximum aggressivity possible on curve.

Thanks to LPP for daily testing and friendship, but I especially thank my team Zero3Spritz for cheering me and supporting me, who helped me reach Top 32 in this tournament and Top 8 at Damnation Fest 2026 two months ago with a very similar Decklist.

#### Game 1

Dario is an incredible player; in this game he has the nuts and I never entered the game.

#### Game 2 and 3

I'm sure these two were among the best matches of the tournament. Both games I won thanks to a mix of pressure with Delver and a good number of counterspells, especially in Game 2, which went long—I saw all four copies. In Game 3, I had a good plan: he begins with Llanowar, so I Gut Shot it and remain open with Force Spike instead of playing Delver.

This sequencing rewards me because I Spike Titania, then play Delver with Brainstorm open on turn 2, cleaning the top, etc.
